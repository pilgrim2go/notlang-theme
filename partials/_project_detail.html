About Second Third

When a few young ladies who had been rescued from sexual slavery in Mumbai’s largest brothel district shared their stories with us on one of our trips, it impressed again on us how poverty has many expressions of abuse but none are perhaps as devastating to the human heart as slavery.

These women and girls have endured unthinkable abuse at the vulnerable ages of fourteen, twelve, even ten. When they share their stories it is with the hope of bringing an end to the inhumane world of human trafficking; so that more girls and women who are trapped may have a chance to find freedom and healing… a chance to hope again.

One girl, Cindy* (we have changed her name), had only been at the rescue home 4 months – after spending over a year in the hospital. We heard how she was sold to a brothel at age 14; how boiling wine was poured over her, and how she had been left to die after her body was taken over by the infection that followed.

Cindy is one of many. We might have been unable to prevent her abuse, but we are able to play a part in her healing. Will you help empower those on the front lines sho are fighting to end sex slavery?

Please take the time to watch Anita's story below and remember the girls still trapped in forced prostitution and slavery in Asia's largest red-light district. Anita was trafficked when she was 19, and now works to love and restore girls like Cindy.

We can be part of stopping it for other young ladies… for someone’s daughter. There is a team who is ready to rescue. They know this kind of slavery and many have experienced it first hand. They know how to reach in, help and restore dignity to a life in need of God’s healing. The team is ready to walk daily towards that new life and hope alongside each young lady who is rescued. They know the culture and the needs.

The outreach team needs your support. They need your prayers and the finances to provide medical care, counseling, education , vocational training, and basic physical needs during the process of rebuilding lives. We have a great opportunity to help bring new life and hope to many young ladies. With every girl that learns to smile, dream, and live again, we are keeping the devil from writing the last word over their lives.
